Popular Destinations

Kandy

Nestled in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, Kandy is a city rich in culture and history. Known as the last royal capital of Sri Lanka, it is home to the iconic Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic. Surrounded by lush greenery and scenic mountains, Kandy offers a tranquil escape with its serene Kandy Lake and vibrant cultural festivals like the Esala Perahera.

Galle

A stunning coastal city on Sri Lanka's southern tip, Galle is famous for its UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Galle Fort. Built by the Portuguese and later fortified by the Dutch, this charming colonial-era town is a blend of history and modern elegance. Explore cobblestone streets, quaint cafes, art galleries, and breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

Jaffna

Located in the northern part of Sri Lanka, Jaffna is a region rich in Tamil culture and heritage. Known for its vibrant temples like the Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, Jaffna offers a unique experience with its traditional cuisine, beautiful lagoons, and serene islands such as Delft Island.
